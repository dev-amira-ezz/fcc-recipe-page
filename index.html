Waffles

Enjoy this waffle recipe. It takes less than 30 minutes to prepare and cook.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • ⅓ cup melted butter
  • 1 ½ cups warm milk

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar in a large bowl.
  2. Beat eggs in another bowl.
  3. Gradually add milk, butter, and vanilla while mixing.
  4. Pour into flour bowl, mix until blended.
  5. Pour onto a preheated waffle iron.
  6. Cook until golden and crisp.
  7. Serve immediately.
